Schedule Your Post

    Instructions

    1. Write your post in the text area.

    2. Attach an image by clicking the "Choose File" button (optional).

    3. Pick a date and time to schedule your post using the calendar icon.

    4. Click "Schedule Post" to queue your post for publishing at the selected time.

    5. Click "Post Now" to immediately publish your post.

    6. Preview your post before publishing by clicking the "Preview" button.

    7. Login with Facebook, Twitter, or Instagram to post directly on your social media accounts.
